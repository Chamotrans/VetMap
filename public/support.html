帳戶及資料

登入 VetMap 後，可在「我的 → 帳號設定 → 刪除帳戶」直接刪除帳戶及相關用戶內容，無需聯絡客服。

內容安全

每項社群投稿均須經審核後才公開。你亦可在內容選單舉報不當內容，或封鎖相關用戶。

VetMap Support

For help with sign-in, clinic data, reviews, quotes, reports, or accounts, email vetmap.app@gmail.com.

Signed-in users can delete their account and associated user content in My → Account Settings → Delete Account. Community submissions are reviewed before publication, and users can report content or block abusive users.
